Interactive Treasure Game

Your transport vehicle stops abruptly as you look ahead to a rickety rope bridge that lies above a giant pool of lava. Your driver slowly makes his way across the bridge when all of a sudden, Mara appears shooting beams of lava at the bridge. Your transport vehicle accelerates in speed to avoid any hits from Mara making a sharp right turn out of danger!

Sounds of swarming beetles and hissing snakes are heard as you make your way through the pitch black tunnel, unaware of what dangers lie ahead.
