Interactive Treasure Game

You've discovered the missing pieces to the map leading to an ancient Benegalese temple. The temple, known as The Temple of the Forbidden Eye, is undergoing excavation for archaelogical research, and YOU are tasked with finding what lies beneath the temple deity's Mara's gifts. But, BEWARE! Don't look into the eyes of Mara or endure grave danger!



Are you brave enough to take on the task?
